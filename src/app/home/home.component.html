About us

Mkulima Shupavu is a Kenyan based agricultural start up that seeks to empower Kenyan farmers and agribusiness enterprenuers by financing farmers with low interest loans while providing enterprenuers with access to a large pool of producers and customers for agricultural inputs.

Our Model

1. Farmers register with us through our platform and choose whether they wish to apply for input loans in the onset of the planting season. Successful applicants are trained on how our company works by our Agents and after signing of binding contracts

2. farmers are given inputs in form of loans by our agrochemical Suppliers in their localities. In addition, they are provided with learning materials to use so as to ensure that their crops have high yields.

3 . Buyers register with us where we liase with them on binding supply orders based on crops to be cultivated by our farmers in the incoming season.

4. Agrochemical Suppliers can apply to become part of our trading partners to supply our wide pool of farmers with standard and certified inputs within their stores locations. Suppliers are then paid prompt;y once products are delivered and invoices verified.

Our vision

We envisions Kenyan farmers with easy access to; quality farming inputs, agricultural loans at low interest rates and real time learning of modern agricultural practices for registered farmers. We strive to provide quality produce for our trade partners as well as a ready market for local manufacturers of agricultural inputs.
